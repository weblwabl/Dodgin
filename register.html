Click Here to Download Dodgeball Tournament Registration Template!

Team registrations and/or donations to the tournament may be made online below.

There are three team divisions: Junior High, High School & Adult. Each team may have 6-10 players. There is a 50 team limit for the tournament this year.

Teams may register online through January 31, 2015 (or once the 50 team maximum limit is reached). The registration fee is $225 per team until January 15, 2015 and will increase to $250 per team on January 16, 2015 until registration closes.



Step 1: Team Captains register their team and pay the team registration fee

Step 2: Team Captains register each team member with a $0 registration fee
